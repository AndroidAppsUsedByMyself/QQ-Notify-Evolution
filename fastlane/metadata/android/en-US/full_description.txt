⚠️ Only Android 8.0 (O) and above is supported.

Support the original QQ / Tim / QQ Lite.

<b>Force the decay QQ notification evolve into a modern style, including features as follows：</b>

- Adapted to native sub-channel notifications, and set different prompts for private chats, group chats, special care, and space messages.
- Using native notification volume and vibration, it is highly compatible with smart binds and watches.
- Following Android messaging app notification style best practices, it supports displaying multiple groups of conversations and historical messages.
- Supported as [Nevo](https://www.coolapk.com/apk/com.oasisfeng.nevo) plugin or standalone.
